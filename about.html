About Our Project

For our project, we decided to build an application that allows users to track the various number of crimes that have taken place in Prince George's county. The concept of this project stemmed from us being current residents of College Park who don't know much about crime occurring in the county. Being college students, we tend to feel bubbled on campus, and many of us lack knowledge of the surrounding areas. We focused on highlighting the different crimes that take place each day. The users can either pick one day from February 2- 21 in 2017 or view all crimes during that period. They will then be able to view a map of crimes and a chart with their respective frequencies. We utilized the Prince George's County Open API to make this project possible.
